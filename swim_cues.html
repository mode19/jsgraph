Freestyle Swimming Cues

Body position

  • Head down facing the floor
  • Feet kick at surface - toes point back
  • Extend forward (not too far and lock out, not too little)
  • Body rotation to breath smoother
  • Quick breath sideways and head snaps back down

Catch

  • Start at the right depth - not too deep not too early
  • Arm at 100 degree bend - sightly out to sides - pull back perpendicular to water (with Lats)
